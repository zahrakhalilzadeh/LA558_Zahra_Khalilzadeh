Impacts of Weather Variables on Corn Yield in Iowa

The goal of this project is to develop a web map using Tableau to visualize crop yields in Iowa over time and identify factors that impact corn production such as weather patterns. The project uses data analysis techniques to explore the correlation between crop yields and factors such as average precipitation, solar radiation, and average temperature. By creating a web map that displays crop yields across different counties in Iowa over time, the project aims to identify trends and patterns that could help farmers optimize their crop management practices.
